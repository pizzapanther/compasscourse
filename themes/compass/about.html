About Dr. Jack Klugh

A captivating communicator specializing in topics that help improve human interaction in the marketplace.

Dr. Jack R. Klugh

Dr. Klugh is an ordained minister of the Assemblies of God with a doctor of Ministry in Pastoral Counseling and Psychotherapy. He is a Veteran of the USAF and Army and has served as a community Pastor, a Military Chaplain and a Clinical Chaplain.

Real world experience, paired with his confident and friendly communication style, has made Dr. Klugh a desired speaker. 

His audiences include the U.S. Army Sergeants Major Academy, the ND State College of Science, Healthcare professionals, Chaplain and Pastors' Associations and churches across the country.

Jack came to Christ in Izmir, Turkey while serving in the U.S. Air Force.  There he met Tina, daughter of a Christian USAF colonel, and they were married in 1977. They have served the Lord together for 39 years and have four adult children and six grandchildren. After his discharge from the Air Force, Jack attended a local Bible College, the A/G Theological Seminary, was ordained, and then entered the U.S. Army as a Chaplain.  

Following his Army service, and after assisting as an Associate Pastor, Jack was selected to serve as the Chief of Chaplains with the Fargo VA Healthcare System. During this time, he has also obtained a doctorate in Pastoral Counseling and Psychotherapy.  God has blessed his work, giving him favor with local, regional, and national VA leadership as well as regional church pastors and Christian organizations. He and Tina are active in their home church, River Valley Assembly of God, Dilworth, MN.

As an ordained minister of the Assemblies of God, Jack's first passion is preaching and teaching the Word of God at every opportunity. Also, because of his clinical chaplaincy experience, he has been invited into the forum of medical conferences, colleges, community organizations and churches to speak on leadership, spirituality in the workplace and other practical topics, using biblical principles as his foundation.
