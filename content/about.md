    <p class="paragraph">
      Jack came to Christ in Izmir, Turkey while serving in the U.S. Air
      Force. There he met Tina, daughter of a Christian USAF colonel, and
      they were married in 1977. They have served the Lord together for 39
      years and have four adult children and six grandchildren. After his
      discharge from the Air Force, Jack attended a local Bible College, the
      A/G Theological Seminary, was ordained, and then entered the U.S. Army
      as a Chaplain.
    </p>
    <p class="paragraph">
      Following his Army service, and after assisting as an Associate
      Pastor, Jack was selected to serve as the Chief of Chaplains with the
      Fargo VA Healthcare System. During this time, he has also obtained a
      doctorate in Pastoral Counseling and Psychotherapy. God has blessed
      his work, giving him favor with local, regional, and national VA
      leadership as well as regional church pastors and Christian
      organizations. He and Tina are active in their home church, River
      Valley Assembly of God, Dilworth, MN.
    </p>
    <p class="paragraph">

      passion is preaching and teaching the Word of God at every
      opportunity. Also, because of his clinical chaplaincy experience, he
      has been invited into the forum of medical conferences, colleges,
      community organizations and churches to speak on leadership,
      spirituality in the workplace and other practical topics, using
      biblical principles as his foundation.
